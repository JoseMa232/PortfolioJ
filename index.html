My First Workshop

My first workshop experience was a mix of nervous anticipation and burgeoning excitement.

First Indie Film

Six months. It feels both like a blink and an eternity since the initial spark of an idea.

Here it is: my own, my very first indie film, finally brought to life. The journey has been a relentless climb, a testament to late nights, unwavering belief, and the incredible support of a small but mighty crew who poured their hearts and souls into this vision.

Holding the finished product in my hands, or even just watching it play on a screen, is a surreal experience, a tangible manifestation of countless hurdles overcome and creative battles won.

This isn't just a film; it's a piece of my journey, etched in frames and sound, a proud and exhilarating milestone on this unpredictable path.
